my life...just the part I want to tell you

I guess everyone starts by 'Halo, what's your name?' Tarien. My name is Tarien van der Merwe. I don't like my surname. It's too Afrikaans and even though I am Afrikaans, I'm not always crazy about my culture. I think they are too narrow minded, but I guess it has its good and bad points.

I grew up in about ten different places. They were mostly small places and living in the city now makes me realize how much I didn't like the small town vibe.

Designing or being creative is my talent, slowly becoming a passion as I explore what design is really all about. I would love to make a difference in so many people's lives in some unexpected and big way through my designs, someday soon.

I sing. Singing is my passion and it motivates me to do the impossible. I always say that when I sing my soul vibrates and the satisfaction I get from that is too intense to just ignore. My one big dream is to travel around the world and sing, to be on the stage in front of thousands and even millions of people.

I love my family and friends and will not tolerate unfairness toward them. I honestly think that somehow I got the best of the best, good, real, honest, friends and family who loves and support you to be the best you, you can be. No funny weird stuff.

The following statement might sound strange but it's so true. I am a solid believer of, 'The power of life and death lies in the tongue.' Therefore I try to never tell a lie, I speak only the truth and if what I say is not true at that particular moment, it will be soon. If anyone ever finds me telling a lie, they should feel obligated to take me on about it.

I am a fun person to be around, when I'm not angry. I don't get angry easily but when I do, apparently I'm a 'bitch', so my sister and best friend says (I didn't know that.. and I'm working on it - promise). I am not a very complicated person. I'm mostly just myself and feel very comfortable in my own skin; if you stick around long enough I'll even poop in front of you (hehe). It's been said that I can make the most awkward moments seem so normal.

I don't care about the silly, unnecessary distractions in life such as money, possessions, people's issues and silly comments or even tantrums sometimes. I do care about having fun and being real, no fakeness.

I am a strong and independent girl but also very well able to submit under authority and leadership above me. Some people just telling me what to do, and how to do it, on the other hand, brings out the very stubborn part of me. I usually feel very bad after doing something wrong, especially if I've unfairly offended someone. I always try to go and apologise, even if it's hard.

Then probably the most important part of who I am is the fact that I've given my life completely to Jesus and I love Him with all my heart. He accepted me regardless of anything I did. He loves me unconditionally and nothing I or anyone else can ever do can change that. Every moment I spend with Him surpasses all the good things that have ever happened to me before, every time. And after a really bad day, the way He makes me feel when I go talk to Him about it, is so mind blowing that I will seriously take ten, even worse days, if I can just get that again. It is NOT a feeling, its real and I will love Him forever.
